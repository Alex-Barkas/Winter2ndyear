# MECH 210: Circuits

## Grading Scheme
- **Active Learning Assignments**: 10% (10 assignments)
- **Lab Assignments**: 30% (6 labs)
- **Quizzes**: 5% (4 quizzes)
- **Midterm Exam**: 20%
- **Final Exam**: 35%
  - *Note: You must pass the Final Exam (>50%) to pass the course.*

## Active Learning Assignments (Tutorials)
There are 10 Active Learning deliverables throughout the semester, typically due weekly during tutorials.

## Quizzes
There are 4 quizzes scheduled for Fridays at 4:30 PM in Weeks 3, 5, 9, and 11.

## MECHMania 2.0
