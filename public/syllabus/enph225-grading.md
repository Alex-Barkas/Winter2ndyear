# ENPH 225: Mechanics

## Grading Scheme
- **Quizzes**: 30% (3 quizzes)
- **Assignments**: 12% (Best 6 of 8)
- **In-Class Group Work**: 9% (Best 9 of 12)
- **FeedbackFruits Group Work**: 18% (Best 9 of 12)
- **Final Exam**: 31%

## Assignments
There are 8 assignments in total. The best 6 will count towards your final grade.
Assignments are typically due on Wednesdays at 4:30 PM.

## Quizzes
There are 3 in-person quizzes scheduled for Week 5, Week 8, and Week 11.
Quizzes are held on Tuesdays at 7:00 PM.

## Group Work
There are regular in-class group work sessions and FeedbackFruits peer reviews. The best 9 of 12 for each category will count.
